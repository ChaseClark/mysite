Simply Classic Events

Making every event Simply Classic

Simply Classic Events is an event planning and management firm serving Allegany, Mineral, and Garrett County.

Owners Billie Jean Clark and Ebone McDaniel, met in 2011 while attending Frostburg State University. After graduating, the two began their lives and careers on opposite sides of the state. When a career opportunity brought the two back to the same area, some major life changes prompted the idea to start Simply Classic Events. There is a combined 10 years of customer service experience along with planning, management, and marketing experience. Paired with passion and commitment Billie Jean and Ebone can guarantee your event is in good hands with Simply Classic Events.
